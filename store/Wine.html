Discover Our Wine Categories

Welcome to our website, where you can explore the diverse and delicious world of Israeli wines. Our wine collection includes three main categories: red, white, and rose, each with its own unique flavor profile and characteristics. We are proud to offer a range of high-quality wines made from the finest grapes grown in Israel's premier wine regions. Whether you're a seasoned wine connoisseur or a curious beginner, we have something for everyone.

Red Wines

Our red wine category features a selection of rich, full-bodied wines that showcase the best of Israeli winemaking. Made from grapes grown in the sun-drenched vineyards of Israel's top wine regions, our red wines are known for their bold flavors, complex aromas, and smooth tannins.

  • Cabernet Sauvignon: Our Cabernet Sauvignon is a full-bodied wine with notes of blackcurrant, cedar, and vanilla. It pairs well with grilled meats, hearty stews, and aged cheeses.
  • Merlot: Our Merlot is a smooth and velvety wine with flavors of black cherry, plum, and chocolate. It's a great choice for pasta dishes, roasted vegetables, and soft cheeses.
  • Syrah: Our Syrah is a spicy and robust wine with hints of black pepper, tobacco, and dark fruit. It's a perfect match for grilled lamb, game meats, and spicy dishes.

White Wines

Our white wine category features a selection of crisp and refreshing wines that showcase the best of Israeli winemaking. Made from grapes grown in the cool, mountainous regions of Israel, our white wines are known for their bright acidity, floral aromas, and fruity flavors.

  • Chardonnay: Our Chardonnay is a rich and buttery wine with notes of apple, pear, and vanilla. It's a perfect match for creamy pasta dishes, roasted chicken, and seafood.
  • Sauvignon Blanc: Our Sauvignon Blanc is a zesty and aromatic wine with flavors of grapefruit, lime, and grass. It pairs well with light salads, grilled fish, and goat cheese.
  • Riesling: Our Riesling is a sweet and floral wine with hints of honey, apricot, and peach. It's a great choice for spicy dishes, Asian cuisine, and blue cheese.

Rose Wines

Our rose wine category features a selection of refreshing and light wines that showcase the best of Israeli winemaking. Made from grapes grown in the sunny vineyards of Israel, our rose wines are known for their delicate aromas, bright acidity, and fruity flavors.

  • Grenache Rose: Our Grenache Rose is a dry and crisp wine with notes of strawberry, raspberry, and watermelon. It's a perfect match for grilled vegetables, light pasta dishes, and fresh salads.
  • Syrah Rose: Our Syrah Rose is a fruity and medium-bodied wine with flavors of cherry, pomegranate, and spice. It pairs well with grilled meats, spicy dishes, and Mediterranean cuisine.
  • Mourvedre Rose: Our Mourvedre Rose is a light and floral wine with hints of citrus, peach, and rose petals. It's a great choice for seafood, summer salads, and soft cheeses.
