Welcome to Our Beer Categories Page

At our website, we are proud to offer a wide selection of beers in Israel, including three main categories: Alt, Lager, and Home Brewing. We import most of our beers, but also offer a selection of locally brewed beers as well. We invite you to explore our beer categories and find the perfect beer for your taste. Whether you are a beer enthusiast or a casual drinker, we have something for everyone. Below you will find more information about each category and the types of beer we offer.

Alt Beer

Alt beer is a German-style beer that is brewed using top-fermenting yeast and fermented at a slightly higher temperature than other types of beer. This gives it a unique flavor profile that is both malty and hoppy. Our selection of alt beers includes:

  • Schumacher Alt: a classic Düsseldorf-style alt beer with a deep amber color and a rich, malty flavor.
  • Diebels Alt: a slightly lighter alt beer that is brewed with more hops, giving it a crisp, bitter finish.
  • Uerige Alt: a strong, full-bodied alt beer with a complex flavor profile that includes notes of caramel, chocolate, and dark fruit.

Lager Beer

Lager beer is a type of beer that is brewed using bottom-fermenting yeast and fermented at a cooler temperature than other types of beer. This results in a clean, crisp flavor profile that is highly carbonated. Our selection of lager beers includes:

  • Paulaner Original Münchner Hell: a classic Bavarian-style lager with a golden color and a light, refreshing flavor.
  • Warsteiner Premium Verum: a premium German lager that is brewed using only the finest ingredients, resulting in a crisp, clean finish.
  • Bitburger Premium Pils: a highly carbonated pilsner-style lager with a bitter, hoppy finish.

Home Brewing

If you're interested in brewing your own beer at home, we offer a selection of home brewing supplies and kits. Our home brewing selection includes:

  • Brewing equipment: including fermenters, airlocks, bottling buckets, and more.
  • Ingredients: including hops, malt extracts, yeast, and brewing sugars.
  • Kits: including complete brewing kits that include everything you need to brew your own beer at home.
